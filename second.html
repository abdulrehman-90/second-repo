Bullying and Its Impact on Child's Mental Health

INTRODUCTION

Bullying is a pivotal concern in children and young people across the globe, and has profound impacts on their psychological well-being. These could be physical, in the form of threats or use of force, verbal, as in name calling, social, which entails rejection of the targeted person or group, and online bullying. Learning about the psychological consequences of this phenomenon is important to help parents, teachers, and politicians assess the possible ways of combating against bullying.
